About Me

A 'purpose-built' Post-graduate degree in Entomology might teach subjects such as Medical and veterinary Entomology, Chemical Ecology, and Molecular Biology, but applying this knowledge effectively in the professional ground is a distinct discipline.

I am a research Entomologist working to understand how biological systems adapt and survive. My expertise spans project design, data analysis, and the development of sustainable research tools.

Beyond science, I am a General Manager in Revenue Cycle Management, optimizing operational efficiency in healthcare. I am also a thinker and musician, bridging the analytical with the artistic.
